Student Fees & Tuition

Fiscal Year 2026

Mandatory Fee Breakdown

Mandatory Fee: UT Dallas’ mandatory student fee revenue totals to $118.4M. The largest contributor is the Information Technology Fee ($29.9M, 25.3%), followed by the Advising Fee ($15.3M, 12.9%) and Infrastructure Fee ($14.7M, 12.4%).

Program, Course Related & Other Fees Breakdown

Program Fee: Program Fee results in a revenue of $10.2M. The Application Fee is the largest source ($3.2M, 31.3%), followed by the Global MBA Bootcamp and Student Teaching Fee (both $1.3M) and the Credit Card Service Fee ($1.2M). All other fees, such as instructional labs, training programs, and departmental charges, each make up less than 5%, showing revenue is concentrated in a few key areas.

Tuition and Student Fees Revenue Overview

Comparison of 4 Categories: When comparing the four main revenue sources for 2026, Net Tuition leads at $369M, followed by Mandatory Fees at $118.4M. Overall, revenue is heavily concentrated in tuition and mandatory fees.
